Vehicle Recognition

This project was done under Prof. Gaurav Pandey at IIT Kanpur to design a framework that can recognise vehicle using the images of number plates. A trivial way to do it is to do OCR on the number plate image and find the vehicle registration number. The problem with this method is that fails to work on the Indian vehicle where the number plates do not have standard font type or size. Also, the condition of the number plate brings the accuracy down even more. And hence this can't be used for implementation of a system that handles most of the corner cases and not just the general one. To find more details about how the problem was approached, feel free to contact me.
